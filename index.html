ABOUT US

Because real estate in Ibiza can be done better.





When we came to the island in 2003, we saw that anyone could call themselves an agent. Many were making quick deals, without really understanding what they were selling. We met people who worked hard all their lives and then bought property here without the right advice. Some made good choices, others did not. We started LET US Ibiza to change that. To bring real knowledge, honesty, and care into every sale. We want people to invest with peace of mind and to enjoy their homes here, knowing they have made a good choice. For their family, for their real estate portfolio. Find our office on the road to San José (Carrer de la tortora 1, bajo 2, 07817, Sant Jordi) Just pass by and have a chat!
