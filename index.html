BOUTIQUE FOCUS

We give our full attention, but only when we really can.

When you decide to work with us, we prefer you to work only with us. It’s not a lifetime contract (you don’t sign anything), just an exclusive focus while we’re on your side. That’s how we can do our job properly with dedication and attention to every detail. Before you visit, we check the paperwork, speak with the architect if needed, and confirm that what’s promised is real.

When something’s off, we say it. Every week, we study 2–3 properties in depth. Doing this for over 10 years has left us with strong knowledge across each hyper local market in Ibiza. What is good value? What is overpriced? So instead of guessing, you’ll know exactly which properties make sense… And which ones to walk away from.

IT HAPPENS MORE OFTEN THAN YOU THINK.

Someone falls in love with a house online, plans a viewing, and decides to buy.

Only later they discover that part of the home was built without a permit, or that the land doesn’t match with the papers. Fixing it (if possible) costs months, and sometimes tens of thousands of euros. It pays to have the right people by your side from day one. As members of the API Real Estate Association, we can access the full market, filter out risky listings in an early stage, and connect you with the right legal and technical experts to check every detail before you sign.

THE DAY FINALLY COMES.

Keys on the table, one last signature and the home is yours.

Now it’s just you, your people, and the island.

Time to get local, settle in, and start living the Ibiza life you came here for.
